Uniforms can not be donated to charities. However, did you know our uniforms could still be recycled or reused? REVAMP is to provide services for SPSS's students for recycling their uniforms. With a few clicks on your fingertips, we can find a new owner for your uniform.

Students are allowed to donate or get fitted uniforms free through this website. Items presented on the website will be well-kept. We provide trusty admins for giving a high-quality experience to all. Students could get their uniforms in our school office. Our main goal is to benefit our students and reduce garment waste.

We will further expand the variety of clothes in the future. Please stay tuned for that.
